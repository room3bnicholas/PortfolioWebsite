ByteFlash

This project centers on developing an affordable and programmable games console designed to align with the UK Government’s Electronics in Schools Initiative, specifically targeting Year 8 students (aged 12–13). The console is built around two core principles: accessibility for educational use and hands-on engagement with STEM concepts. By combining a user-friendly interface, modifiable code, and low-cost hardware, the device aims to demystify electronics and programming while fostering creativity and problem-solving skills in young learners.
